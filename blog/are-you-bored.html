Are you bored? Well not this board

Co-op between SCOC and International Board

Posted by Maria Kouneli on 2nd April, 2017
Estimated reading time: 2 minutes

There are 3 weeks left until Spring Congress – the most important event of EESTEC! It is undoubtfully the highlight of every mandate, the meeting point of the whole Europe, the core of the Association, the incubator of new ideas and changes, and the affair where the future of EESTEC is shaped.

This year, Spring Congress is quite different for me. For the first time, I am looking at it from the perspective of a Board member. Spring Congress might be a one time event for most of EESTEC but for me and the SCOC it is an ongoing process, that lasts throughout the whole year. It is an important milestone for my mandate and a moment that will mark the beginning of a new journey for a new Board.

Working with SCOC was a pleasure! They showed unconditional commitment to the event and passion to do the best they could. A year ago, they applied for a minimal Congress but the Spring Congress that they are preparing is far from minimal! LC Ljubljana stepped up when no other LC was willing to and they prepared one of the greatest Congresses in the history of the Association! Working with them has made me feel that I have nothing to worry about, that they have thought about every small detail to make this Congress epic! I am sincerely grateful for having the opportunity to work with such an amazing team!

See you in Ljubljana!
