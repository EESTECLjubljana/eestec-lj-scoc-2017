EESTEC

Electrical Engineering Students' European Association is an apolitical, non-governmental and non-profit organization which targets and trains through its activities students from electrical profile Universities, Institutes and Technical Schools in Europe. The association was founded in The Netherlands, in 1986. Nowadays, EESTEC is present in 23 countries and 49 universities across Europe with over 4000 members of whom 3000 are currently active, and indirectly, EESTEC is addressing all students in the faculties that EESTEC exists. More...

Vision

Uniting Electrical Engineering and Computer Science students all over the Europe in order to reach their full potential in academic and social life

Mission

To spread and strengthen a network of students across Europe and develop an international collaborative environment, where the expansion of academic knowledge and development of soft skills is encouraged.

Marand

Marand d.o.o. is a Slovenian based company that is operating in ICT markets. Being predominately software development house they are present in healthcare from 1990 and well recognized as a strong investor in their own R&D capacity. Their healthcare strategy is underpinned with the statement Integrate.Open.Innovate.

Marand’s solutions are IHE certified and use best in class integration platforms to integrate the data from various sources, including the device data into a single view of the patient record.

The clinical data repository with associated tools follows openEHR methodology which is world’s leading clinical data modelling foundation, built by clinicians, fully in public domain and by having the data in well-defined non-proprietary format. Therefore, the choice of end user applications and innovation on top of the platform is endless.

Marand is constantly changing state of IT solutions in healthcare and invest time and energy in getting new knowledge and experience, encourage innovation and teamwork.

One of the main goals is to bring bright minds and great ideas together in a stimulating and challenging environment. Interaction and interchange of ideas, thoughts, and experiences to address some of the most pressing medical issues are reasons for Medrock week where great learning, networking opportunity, new friendships and innovative ideas become reality for everyone who is not afraid of a challenge how to disrupt the way traditional healthcare data is managed and stored by using block chain, find patterns in data, use state of the art tools and try to figure out what are the best ways and tools to ease the gap in communications between the care team and the patient.

Medrock Week is a week-long event where innovative developers, designers, and business people come together to find solutions to front-line healthcare challenges. The event is free to attend but everyone is required to apply and be accepted due to a limited number of spots.

For more information on Medrock week 2017, visit www.medrockweek.com.

We believe everyone can play a role in the future of healthcare!

Hal mBills

MBILLS is a driving force in the area of mobile payment in the region. We developed Hal mBills – an innovative platform for mobile payment that is creating payment options, which have never been seen before. Hal mBills is based on the mobile wallet, which replaces the commonly used cash wallet. Untill now we could buy anything with cash, but from now on we will be able to pay all those products and services with e-money. Hal mBills enables users to pay receipts and household bills, to shop online, make peer to peer transactions and even to order and pay ahead for products and services just by scanning a QR code. Customers can do all this with their mobile phone 24/7/365 in real time: fast, safe, easy and affordable for the company and for the end consumer. Hal mBills platform works on its own, but connects with banking and card systems and is based on the current companies’ infrastructure. MBILLS is the only Slovenian company so far that has an authorisation from the Bank of Slovenia to perform electronic money issuing services. This autorization is the gateaway for the whole European Union. In 2016 our platform Hal mBills received the e-Award for the best IT project from the Slovenian IT Organization, which we are very proud of.
